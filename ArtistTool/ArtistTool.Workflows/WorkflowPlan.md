## Marketing plan

Go-to market plan. 

- Single edition, or 
- multiple print recommendations. 
 
Choose one.

- For each: 
    - canvas, 
    - metal, 
    - acrylic, 
    - traditional framed photograph
- Do this:
    - Generate preview
    - Pros and cons for the medium
    - Price research

Choose the mediums and prices.

Generate the sales page for each: ad copy, price, images
